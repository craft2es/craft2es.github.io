The Shadow Banks of the Barrio

Loans outside the formal banking system with an interest rates higher than 16 percent are illegal in New York. Informal moneylenders can charge up to a 900 percent interest rate.

By Alma Sacasa, Constanza Gallardo y Mónica Cordero
